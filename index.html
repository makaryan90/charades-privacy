Privacy Policy - Charades Fun

Effective Date: December 7, 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to Charades Fun! This privacy policy explains how our mobile application ("App") collects, uses, and protects your information.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Video Recordings: When you use the optional video recording feature, videos are stored locally on your device only. We do not upload, access, or store these videos on our servers.
  • Game Data: Temporary gameplay information (words played, scores) used solely for app functionality during your game session.

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Identifiers: Anonymous device IDs used for advertising and purchase verification
  • App Interactions: How you use the app (buttons clicked, features used) to improve our service
  • Crash Reports: Anonymous technical data when the app crashes to help us fix bugs

3. How We Use Your Information

  • App Functionality: To provide game features and video recording
  • Advertising: To show relevant ads through Google AdMob
  • Purchase Verification: To validate premium subscriptions through RevenueCat
  • Analytics: To understand how users interact with the app and improve it
  • Bug Fixes: To identify and resolve technical issues

4. Information Sharing

We share limited information with the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob: Device IDs and ad interaction data for serving advertisements. AdMob Privacy Policy
  • RevenueCat: Purchase data for managing premium subscriptions. RevenueCat Privacy Policy
  • Google Play Services: For app distribution and in-app purchases. Google Privacy Policy

We do NOT sell your personal information to third parties.

5. Data Security

  • All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using industry-standard protocols (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Videos are stored locally on your device with standard Android security
  • We do not store sensitive personal information on our servers

6. Your Rights and Choices

  • Video Deletion: Delete recorded videos anytime from your device's storage
  • Opt-Out of Ads: Purchase premium subscription to remove ads
  • Data Deletion: Uninstall the app to remove all locally stored data
  • Account Deletion: Not applicable - we don't require user accounts

7. Children's Privacy

Our app is rated for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

8. No User Accounts

Charades Fun does not require users to create accounts, sign in, or provide personal identification information. You can use the app immediately after installation.

9. Data Retention

  • Videos: Stored on your device until you delete them
  • Game Data: Temporary session data, cleared after each game
  • Analytics Data: Aggregated and anonymized, retained for up to 26 months
  • Crash Logs: Retained for up to 90 days for debugging purposes

10. International Data Transfers

Your information may be processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for any international data transfers.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Third-Party Links

Our app may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties. Please review their privacy policies.

13. California Privacy Rights

California residents have additional rights under CCPA. We do not sell personal information. You may request information about data collection by contacting us below.

14. GDPR Compliance (EU Users)

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have rights under GDPR including:

  • Right to access your data
  • Right to rectification
  • Right to erasure
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to data portability

Contact us to exercise these rights.
